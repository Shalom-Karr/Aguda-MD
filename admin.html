Formatting tips

You don't need to memorize anything — just use the toolbar buttons. But here are the keyboard shortcuts if you prefer them:

Bold Ctrl+B
Italic Ctrl+I
Link Ctrl+K
Save Ctrl+S
Insert imageClick 🖼, paste, or drag-drop

How it works

Select some text first, then press the shortcut (or click the toolbar button). The formatting wraps around your selection. Stars (**) and other markers will show in the editor — those become bold/italic/headings on the live site.
